about=
    The Feature Transfer Tool is a powerful and user-friendly QGIS plugin that enables seamless copying and pasting of features between layers. Designed with efficiency and ease of use in mind, this tool ensures that feature data is accurately transferred, maintaining both geometry and attributes. Users have the flexibility to copy all fields in the target layers or select specific fields, tailoring the transfer process to their needs.
